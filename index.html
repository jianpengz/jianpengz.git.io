Biography

I received my PhD degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), China, in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Yong Xia. I was also a research fellow at the Australia Institute for Machine Learning (AIML), University of Adelaide (UoA), Australia, jointly work with Dr. Johan Verjans. Before that, I was a visitor at UoA under the supervision of Prof. Chunhua Shen.

My research interests mainly focus on deep learning technologies for medical image analysis, especially annotation-limited 3D medical image segmentation.
